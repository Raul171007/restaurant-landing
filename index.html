Our story

A Family Tradition
Since 1987

La Piazza was founded by the Rossi family with a simple mission: bring the true flavors of Rome to your table. Three generations later, we still use the same recipes, the same passion and the same love for good food.

Every dish is prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients combined with imported Italian products. We believe great food brings people together.
